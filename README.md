# 🌱 Clean Habits, Healthy Surroundings

A modern web application designed to promote **environmental awareness, hygiene practices, and sustainable living** through interactive and engaging content.

🔗 Live Website: https://clean-surroundings.netlify.app/

---

## 📌 Overview

"Clean Habits, Healthy Surroundings" is a community-focused web platform that educates users about maintaining cleanliness, improving personal hygiene, and protecting the environment.

The project emphasizes the importance of daily habits in building a cleaner and healthier society. Research shows that structured cleaning routines and awareness significantly improve living conditions and reduce health risks. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## 🎯 Objectives

- Promote awareness about environmental cleanliness

## 🌍 Why This Project Matters

Environmental sustainability and hygiene are critical global concerns. Poor sanitation and lack of awareness contribute to pollution, health issues, and environmental degradation.

This project aims to:

- Simplify complex environmental concepts  
- Make awareness accessible to everyone  
- Encourage small habits that lead to big impact
